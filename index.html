About MOJAK

Mojak is all about people and feels. Born from the classic Mojak meme—the “Feels Guy” who’s been part of internet culture for years—it’s now grown into something bigger on the Ethereum blockchain. No central team, no gatekeepers—just a group of people who vibe with the same meme spirit and want to build together. Cheap, fast, and open on Ethereum, MOJAK is where culture meets crypto, powered by everyone who knows that feel.
